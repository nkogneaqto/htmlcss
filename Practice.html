The Toyota Sera (my dream project car) is a 3-door 2+2 hatchback coupe
manufactured and marketed by Toyota from 1990 to 1996.
The Sera debuted in 1988 as the Toyota AXV-II concept car in a near production-ready form,
and is noted for its mostly glass roof canopy and its butterfly doors,
which tilt up and forward when open.
The name "Sera" is Italian for evening and is French and Spanish for "it will be".
